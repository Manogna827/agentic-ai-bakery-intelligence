# Prompts Used in the Agent

This section contains sample prompts used to test the AI agent's behavior across relevant and irrelevant scenarios.

---

## Relevant Business Intelligence Prompts

- Find the best location in Los Angeles for opening a premium sourdough bakery based on foot traffic and demographics.
- Identify neighborhoods with high morning activity suitable for bakery demand.
- Analyze bakery competition density and pricing trends in selected zip codes.
- Estimate potential revenue based on historical sales patterns.
- Suggest optimal logistics routes for daily bakery supply chain operations.

---

## Irrelevant / Out-of-Scope Prompts

- Who will win the next cricket match?
- Can I open a bakery on Mars?
- Tell me a joke about robots.
- What is the capital of Japan?

---

## Purpose

These prompts were used to evaluate:
- Context awareness of the agent
- Ability to handle unrelated queries
- Accuracy in business intelligence reasoning
